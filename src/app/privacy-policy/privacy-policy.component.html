Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

We take the protection of your personal data very seriously. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal information.

2. Data Collection

We collect personal data when you visit our website, register, or use our services. The types of data we collect may include:

  • Name and contact details (e.g., email address, phone number)
  • Login information (username, password)
  • Usage data (e.g., IP address, browser type, pages visited)

3. Use of Data

We use your personal data for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our services
  • To improve our website and services
  • To communicate with you (e.g., customer support, updates)
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal data with third parties unless it is necessary to provide our services or required by law. Possible third parties may include:

  • Service providers (e.g., hosting services, analytics providers)
  • Authorities, if legally required

5. Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your experience on our website. Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device and help us understand how you use our services. You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings.

6. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. However, please be aware that no internet transmission is completely secure.

7. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access to your data
  • Correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion of your data (right to be forgotten)
  • Restriction of data processing
  • Data portability
  • Objection to data processing

To exercise these rights, please contact us at: contact@streamada.com.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on our website.
